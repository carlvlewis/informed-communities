Collaboration playbooks

Our TDE Playbooks series is a closer look at several of the tactics included in our TDE guide.

Our TDE playbooks are designed to provide a closer look at what TDE looks like in real communities. These ideas are built off of real-world examples of events or strategies that data providers have implemented to better connect residents to open data.

Although we have included these playbooks on this Action tools page as tactics to use during the Action phase, you may consider using them during the Discovery phase as well. The tactics described in these playbooks are versatile — just like the rest of the TDE approach — and should be used creatively.
